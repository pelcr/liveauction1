About Us

Launching on January 1, 2025, Live Auction is a revolutionary peer-to-peer investment platform where members can buy and sell LT shares to make profits. Join our community and take control of your financial future!

How It Works

  1. Create an account and verify your details.
  2. Purchase LT shares using cash.
  3. Hold your investment for a 3-day cycle.
  4. After maturity, shares are automatically listed in the marketplace for new buyers.
  5. Track all transactions and reinvest for continuous growth.
